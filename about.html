About This Platform

This platform is a student-developed academic information portal created to simplify access to essential college-related information. It provides a centralized space where students can easily find academic notices, campus events, and previous year question papers.

Our Mission

To support students by providing timely and accurate academic information in one place, helping them stay focused on their academic journey.

What We Offer

  • • Centralized academic notices
  • • Sem-wise PYQs collection
  • • Technical & Cultural events
  • • Clean & Responsive UI
